Primary Color (#0083c6) is a bright blue used for all page headers and footers.

Secondary Color (#ebffdd) is a very pale green-white color used for page content and page title/footer fonts.

Accent Color 1 (#82bc00) is a bright green used for font and icon/logo accents.

Accent Color 2 (#023e58) is a navy blue used for the navigation bar and borders.
