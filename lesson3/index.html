Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide current and forecasted weather to citizens and travelers of the Sherman and Paris Peaks and surrounding areas in Southeastern Idaho.

Site Name

The name chosen for this site is peaksweather.com. The name references the fact that it services two prominent mountain peaks and their surrounding areas.
